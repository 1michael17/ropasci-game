How to play

  1. Click on START button
  2. Click on either: Rock / Paper / Scissors
  3. if Computer = 'Rock' and You = 'Paper' or
  4. if Computer = 'Paper' and You = 'Scissors' or
  5. if Computer = 'Scissors' and You = 'Rock'
  6. Draw doesn't count
  7. The FIRST to get 5 points Wins
